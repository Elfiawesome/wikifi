Elara Voss

Elara Voss is a Luminal Weaver and a senior member of the Order of Dawn. Born in the lower districts of City of Lume, she taught herself magic from salvaged texts before being recruited by the Order at age sixteen.

Early Life

Elara grew up in the Ashwood district, the daughter of a cartographer father who disappeared when she was nine. She believes his disappearance is connected to the sealed vaults beneath City of Lume, a theory that drives much of her investigative work.

Abilities

Elara specialises in Luminal Weaving — the art of bending ambient light into tangible force. Her constructs are known for their precision rather than raw power.

"I don't need to hit hard. I need to hit right."

She carries the Dagger of Echoes, an artifact recovered from The Hollow that vibrates in the presence of active illusion magic.

Relationships

Her closest ally is Kade Morrow, whose complementary skill set has made them an effective field pair. Their relationship is professional but warm, built on years of shared missions.

She maintains a cautious respect for the Shadow Syndicate, believing that understanding them is more valuable than opposing them blindly.

Appearances

Elara features prominently in the second and third arcs of the main story, serving as the primary investigator into the disappearances plaguing The Hollow.
