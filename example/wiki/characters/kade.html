Kade Morrow

Kade Morrow is a Sentinel of the Order of Dawn and field partner to Elara Voss. He is originally from the Northern Reaches and joined the Order after the destruction of his home village, an attack he attributes to operatives of the Shadow Syndicate.

Background

Kade is a man of few words and considerable precision. Where Elara investigates, Kade acts. Where she plans for five outcomes, he plans for two — the one where things go well and the one where they don't.

Combat Style

Unlike the magic-focused members of the Order of Dawn, Kade relies on physical skill and tactical awareness. He trained under Commander Vael in the Order's Northern Keep and holds the rank of Senior Sentinel — the highest field rank beneath the Circle of Wardens.

His preferred weapons are twin short swords named Dusk and Dawn, forged from tempered lumenite ore mined beneath City of Lume.

Role in the Story

Kade serves as the main point of view character for the first arc, before the narrative expands to include Elara Voss and the wider world of Lume.
