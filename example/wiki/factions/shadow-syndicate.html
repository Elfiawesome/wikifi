Shadow Syndicate

The Shadow Syndicate is a secretive organisation operating across Lume and beyond. Little is verified — most information comes from intercepted communications and the accounts of defectors.

What Is Known

The Syndicate deals in three things: forbidden artefacts, esoteric knowledge, and influence. It is believed to have operatives at every level of Lume's society, including (according to Elara Voss) within the Order of Dawn itself.

The Hollow Connection

Kade Morrow holds the Syndicate responsible for the destruction of his home village. The evidence is circumstantial but consistent with known Syndicate methods — specifically their use of corrupted lumen fields as weapons.

Evidence from The Hollow suggests the Syndicate has been excavating beneath the district, possibly searching for the same sealed vaults that interest Elara Voss.

Ideology

Where the Order of Dawn seeks to protect Lume from dangerous magic, the Syndicate seeks to use it — regardless of consequence.
