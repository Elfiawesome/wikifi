The Hollow

The Hollow

The Hollow is the abandoned lower district of City of Lume, located beneath the Ashwood neighbourhood at the base of the Citadel Ridge. It was evacuated following the Hollow Incident approximately forty years ago and remains officially off-limits by order of the Order of Dawn.

The Hollow Incident

The circumstances of the evacuation are poorly documented in public records. What is known: over a period of six months, more than sixty residents were reported missing or found dead under unusual circumstances. The Order of Dawn declared the area a magical hazard zone and sealed the entrances.

Elara Voss suspects the incident was connected to Shadow Syndicate excavation activities in the district's underground network.

Current Status

Despite the official closure, the Hollow is regularly accessed by scavengers, researchers, and agents of unknown affiliation. The Shadow Syndicate is believed to maintain at least one operational base in the lower tunnels.

Kade Morrow and Elara Voss led a joint investigation into the Hollow during the second arc, discovering the remains of a lumenite processing facility that predated the city's official records.

Layout

The Hollow consists of:

  • Surface level — Collapsed buildings and overgrown streets
  • Sub-level 1 — Old sewers and utility tunnels, partially flooded
  • Sub-level 2 — Pre-Shattering foundations, sealed vault doors
  • Sub-level 3 — Unknown. Believed inaccessible without specialised tools

Significance

The Hollow is widely considered the most important unresolved mystery in City of Lume. Whatever was sealed beneath it has drawn the attention of both the Order of Dawn and the Shadow Syndicate for decades.
