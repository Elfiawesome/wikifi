## What is Lume?

**Lume** is a world of ancient magic, warring factions, and forgotten gods. This wiki documents its history, characters, and places for readers, players, and storytellers alike.

## Getting Started

Explore the world of Lume through its key pillars:

- **Characters** — The people who shape Lume's fate, from heroes to villains
- **Factions** — The great organisations that vie for power
- **World** — The cities, ruins, and wildernesses of Lume
