# Shadow Syndicate

The **Shadow Syndicate** is a secretive organisation operating across Lume and beyond. Little is verified — most information comes from intercepted communications and the accounts of defectors.

## What Is Known

The Syndicate deals in three things: **forbidden artefacts**, **esoteric knowledge**, and **influence**. It is believed to have operatives at every level of Lume's society, including (according to [[characters/elara]]) within the [[factions/order-of-dawn]] itself.

## The Hollow Connection

[[characters/kade]] holds the Syndicate responsible for the destruction of his home village. The evidence is circumstantial but consistent with known Syndicate methods — specifically their use of corrupted lumen fields as weapons.

Evidence from [[world/the-hollow]] suggests the Syndicate has been excavating beneath the district, possibly searching for the same sealed vaults that interest [[characters/elara]].

## Ideology

Where the [[factions/order-of-dawn]] seeks to protect Lume from dangerous magic, the Syndicate seeks to *use* it — regardless of consequence.
