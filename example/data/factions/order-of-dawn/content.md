# Order of Dawn

The **Order of Dawn** is one of the oldest institutions in [[world/city-of-lume]], founded three centuries ago in the aftermath of the Shattering. Its mandate is the protection of Lume from magical threats, rogue artefacts, and the machinations of the [[factions/shadow-syndicate]].

## History

The Order was established by the Seven Founders, mages and warriors who survived the Shattering and vowed to prevent its recurrence. For most of its history the Order operated transparently, embedded within the city's governance.

That relationship fractured approximately forty years ago during the **Hollow Incident**, after which the Order became increasingly autonomous and secretive — a change that remains controversial.

## Structure

Members of the Order are ranked from Initiate through to Adept, Sentinel, Warden, and finally the elite **Circle of Wardens** at the top. Each rank has clearly delineated responsibilities and privileges.

## Relations with the Shadow Syndicate

The Order views the [[factions/shadow-syndicate]] as its primary adversary, though key members such as [[characters/elara]] advocate for intelligence gathering over open conflict.

> "You cannot fight what you do not understand." — Elara Voss

## Headquarters

The Order operates from the Citadel, a fortified tower in the upper district of [[world/city-of-lume]].
