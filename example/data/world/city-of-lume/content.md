# City of Lume

The **City of Lume** is the largest settlement in the known world and the political, magical, and economic centre of the region. It sits at the mouth of the Vael River on a wide coastal plain, with the elevated Citadel Ridge rising in its northern quarter.

## Districts

The city is divided into six major districts:

| District | Character |
|---|---|
| Citadel Ridge | Administrative and military. Home of the [[factions/order-of-dawn]] |
| Merchant Quarter | Commerce, banking, and the Lumenite Exchange |
| Ashwood | Working class. Birthplace of [[characters/elara]] |
| The Hollow | Abandoned lower district. Site of mysterious disappearances |
| Docklands | Trade hub, busy and chaotic |
| Old Lume | Ancient ruins, partially excavated |

## The Hollow

[[world/the-hollow]] is a district of particular significance — a formerly populated area that was evacuated after a series of unexplained events. [[characters/elara]] believes it holds answers to her father's disappearance.

## History

Lume was founded in the immediate aftermath of the Shattering by survivors who established the post-Shattering calendar with Year 0 marking the city's founding. The [[factions/order-of-dawn]] was established in Year 12, when it became clear that remnants of the catastrophe still lurked across the region.

## Magic Infrastructure

The city runs on **lumenite** — a crystallised magical mineral mined from veins deep beneath the city's foundations. Its extraction and trade are managed by the Lumenite Exchange, which makes Lume one of the wealthiest cities in the known world.
