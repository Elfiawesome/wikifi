# The Hollow

**The Hollow** is the abandoned lower district of [[world/city-of-lume]], located beneath the Ashwood neighbourhood at the base of the Citadel Ridge. It was evacuated following the **Hollow Incident** approximately forty years ago and remains officially off-limits by order of the [[factions/order-of-dawn]].

## The Hollow Incident

The circumstances of the evacuation are poorly documented in public records. What is known: over a period of six months, more than sixty residents were reported missing or found dead under unusual circumstances. The [[factions/order-of-dawn]] declared the area a magical hazard zone and sealed the entrances.

[[characters/elara]] suspects the incident was connected to [[factions/shadow-syndicate|color:#6b7280]] excavation activities in the district's underground network.

## Current Status

Despite the official closure, the Hollow is regularly accessed by scavengers, researchers, and agents of unknown affiliation. The [[factions/shadow-syndicate]] is believed to maintain at least one operational base in the lower tunnels.

[[characters/kade]] and [[characters/elara]] led a joint investigation into the Hollow during the second arc, discovering the remains of a lumenite processing facility that predated the city's official records.

## Layout

The Hollow consists of:

- **Surface level** — Collapsed buildings and overgrown streets
- **Sub-level 1** — Old sewers and utility tunnels, partially flooded
- **Sub-level 2** — Pre-Shattering foundations, sealed vault doors
- **Sub-level 3** — Unknown. Believed inaccessible without specialised tools

## Significance

The Hollow is widely considered the most important unresolved mystery in [[world/city-of-lume]]. Whatever was sealed beneath it has drawn the attention of both the [[factions/order-of-dawn]] and the [[factions/shadow-syndicate]] for decades.
