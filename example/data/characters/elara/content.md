# Elara Voss

**Elara Voss** is a Luminal Weaver and a senior member of the [[factions/order-of-dawn]]. Born in the lower districts of [[world/city-of-lume]], she taught herself magic from salvaged texts before being recruited by the Order at age sixteen.

## Early Life

Elara grew up in the Ashwood district, the daughter of a cartographer father who disappeared when she was nine. She believes his disappearance is connected to the sealed vaults beneath [[world/city-of-lume]], a theory that drives much of her investigative work.

## Abilities

Elara specialises in **Luminal Weaving** — the art of bending ambient light into tangible force. Her constructs are known for their precision rather than raw power.

> "I don't need to hit hard. I need to hit *right*."

She carries the **Dagger of Echoes**, an artifact recovered from [[world/the-hollow]] that vibrates in the presence of active illusion magic.

## Relationships

Her closest ally is [[characters/kade]], whose complementary skill set has made them an effective field pair. Their relationship is professional but warm, built on years of shared missions.

She maintains a cautious respect for the [[factions/shadow-syndicate]], believing that understanding them is more valuable than opposing them blindly.

## Appearances

Elara features prominently in the second and third arcs of the main story, serving as the primary investigator into the disappearances plaguing [[world/the-hollow]].
