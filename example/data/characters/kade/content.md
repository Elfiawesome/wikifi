# Kade Morrow

**Kade Morrow** is a Sentinel of the [[factions/order-of-dawn]] and field partner to [[characters/elara]]. He is originally from the Northern Reaches and joined the Order after the destruction of his home village, an attack he attributes to operatives of the [[factions/shadow-syndicate]].

## Background

Kade is a man of few words and considerable precision. Where Elara investigates, Kade acts. Where she plans for five outcomes, he plans for two — the one where things go well and the one where they don't.

## Combat Style

Unlike the magic-focused members of the [[factions/order-of-dawn]], Kade relies on physical skill and tactical awareness. He trained under Commander Vael in the Order's Northern Keep and holds the rank of **Senior Sentinel** — the highest field rank beneath the Circle of Wardens.

His preferred weapons are twin short swords named **Dusk** and **Dawn**, forged from tempered lumenite ore mined beneath [[world/city-of-lume]].

## Role in the Story

Kade serves as the main point of view character for the first arc, before the narrative expands to include [[characters/elara]] and the wider world of Lume.
